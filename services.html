Meet the Team

With the entire firm’s expertise at your disposal, you’ll be able to focus on your business and its operations while we take care of your monthly accounting records. Depending on your requirements, VHA Accounting Solutions will provide accounting specialists to work either from our office or at your premises, whether you simply need financial and accounting support or more permanent monthly services.

We offer comprehensive accounting services, including:

  • Capturing of monthly accounting records (up to trial balance)
  • Generating custom reports based on processing
  • VAT201 submissions
  • EMP201 submissions
  • EMP501 bi-annual submissions
  • Payroll processing and payslip generation
  • Generation of management accounts
  • Compilation engagements (Preparation of the annual financial statements)
  • Tailor made solutions for your accounting and bookkeeping needs
  • Central supplier database for government tenders - registration with the Central supplier database for government tenders. Up keep of your information on the database.
  • Application for NCR (National Credit Regulator) certificates
  • Assist clients with appling for business visa applications
