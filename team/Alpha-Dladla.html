Alpha Dladla

Alpha Dladla was born in Pietermaritzburg on 18 September 1989, and schooled in Howick at Haza Primary School and then Mconjwana High School, where he matriculated in 2008.

  • Obtained his National Diploma in Accounting with Durban University of Technology in 2011.
  • started his career as an accounting clerk at Colenbrander Incorporated where he worked for over 5 years.
  • Alpha then joined VHA Accounting Solutions Inc where he serves as a director.

Alpha is a full practicing member of the South African Institute of Professional Accountants as a Professional Accountant (SA). Alpha's speciality is financial statements preparation and bookkeeping to trial balance.
