Vidyanth Bhola

Vidyanth Bhola was born in Greytown on 12 May 1985, and schooled in Greytown at Greytown Primary School and then Greytown Secondary School, where he matriculated in 2002.

Vidyanth obtained his BCom Degree with Varsity College in 2016, Higher diploma in Taxation with UNISA, and post-graduate Accounting Diploma with Varsity College. Vidyanth also provides academic support services to learners and undergraduates. Vidyanth is also a senior tax lecturer at Damelin, he shares a passion for teaching.
